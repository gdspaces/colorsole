ColorSole is a colour-elimination puzzle based on a square grid filled with different colour. ColorSole is a variant of Hitori puzzle created by Japanese publishers Nikoli.

Give your brain a workout.

Rules

The puzzle starts with a grid full of colour and you need to determine which colour to "black out" so that there are no duplicate colour in any row or column.

There are also two more rules determining which colour may be blacked out:

• Rule 1: No empty space can touch any other empty space horizontally or vertically (they may touch diagonally however) 

• Rule 2: The remaining colour cells must form a single connected network path. In other words, you must be able to move from any colour cell to another colour cell moving only horizontally and vertically. 

If any of the above rules are violated then a red border will be shown.

 Moves : 10
